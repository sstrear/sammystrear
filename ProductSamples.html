Sammies – The Ultimate Packable Adventure Shoe

Sammies are designed for outdoor explorers who refuse to compromise on comfort, performance, or portability. Engineered with lightweight, waterproof materials and an innovative collapsible sole, these shoes adapt to any environment—whether you're backpacking through rugged terrain, unwinding at camp, or navigating snowy après-ski moments. The flexible design allows for easy packing without sacrificing durability, while the insulated interior keeps your feet warm and dry in any condition. Sammies redefine adventure footwear by blending functionality, comfort, and modern aesthetics into a shoe that's as versatile as your journey.

Dual-Threaded Bolt – A SolidWorks Experiment in Technical CAD

This project showcases an advanced SolidWorks design of a dual-threaded bolt, demonstrating precision engineering and technical CAD expertise. The bolt features two interwoven thread patterns, a complex geometry that challenges conventional machining and additive manufacturing techniques. This experiment highlights proficiency in parametric modeling, thread customization, and mechanical design principles, pushing the boundaries of standard fastener construction. By exploring the intricacies of multi-threaded components, this SolidWorks model serves as both a technical showcase and a conceptual exploration of non-traditional fastening solutions.

Glass Bottle Insert – Optimized Airflow & Fluid Sealing

This project showcases an advanced design focused on optimizing airflow and fluid sealing. The precise engineering ensures smooth fluid control and controlled pouring without unwanted spills or air bubbles. It is a technical exploration of fluid dynamics and seal optimization.
